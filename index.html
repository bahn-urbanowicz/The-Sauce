What is WildStar?

WildStar is a Sci-Fi MMORPG (Massively Multi-Player Online Role Playing Game) that is being developed by NCsoft at Carbine Studios located in Southern California.

WildStar Experience

Create a character and experience WildStar's epic spacey adventure. Choose from eight races within two factions, six classes and forge your path as a Soldier, Explorer, Settler or Scientist. Quest and do battle across the planet Nexus solo or with allies which will immerse players in WildStar's new Momentum Mechanics.

System Requirements

  • CPU:Intel Pentium Core2 Duo 2.4 GHz, AMD Phenom X3 2.3 GHz
  • RAM: 4GB
  • Graphics Card: NVIDIA GeForce 8800 GT, ATI Radeon HD 3850
  • Operating System: Windows XP SP2 (64-bit) or later.

These requirements are for Beta Testing and may be changed later on
